this is My favorite Movies Of All Time

1.Pisaj - คน ผี ปีศาจ (2004)

One of the best Thai-horror film of all time it's about young girl who losing both of her parents in a horrific tragedy she ends up with her aunt who is a printing house owner and a part-time spiritual medium.

2.Behind the Painting - ข้างหลังภาพ (2001)

It tells the story of Nopphon, a student in Japan, who meets and develops a relationship with lady Kirati, the newly married wife of a family acquaintance. Nopphon and Kirati develop romantic feelings which they are unable to acknowledge, leading to tensions as the characters face the conflict between their feelings and familial duties.

3.The Iron Ladies - สตรีเหล็ก (2000)

A volleyball team made up of gay and transgender players surprise their competitors by winning their way to Thailand's national championships.This LGBT+ film will make you laugh and cry at the same time

4. Nang Nak - นางนาก (1999)

Thai supernatural horror film based on the Thai legend of Mae Nak Phra Khanong. It was directed by Nonzee Nimibutr and released in 1999 by Buddy Film and Video Production Co. in Thailand. It depicts the life of a devoted ghost wife and her unsuspecting husband.
